Have you ever struggled cutting a Mango?
I have a solution for you!! 2 in fact!
Cut the Mango in half, then cut squares
into it with the skin still on.
This next one is much easier to do and is my favorite,
cut the Mango in half again, then get a glass cup.
(Glass cups work the best) Get 1 Mango slice
and slide it down the cup with the skin
on the outside of the cup and the good part inside.
Then, have a yummy Mango!!!
